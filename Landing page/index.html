"Frequently Asked Questions"

What type of audio drivers do these headphones use?

The Crusher Evo is equipped with 40mm drivers with adjustable sensory bass technology.

How long does the battery last?

The Crusher Evo offers up to 40 hours of battery life, and with Rapid Charge, just 10 minutes of charging gives you 4 hours of playback.

Does it support voice assistants?

Yes, the Crusher Evo supports voice assistants like Google Assistant and Siri for hands-free control.

How does the Tile finding technology work?

The built-in Tile technology allows you to locate your headphones using the Tile app. You can ring your headphones, see their last known location, and even get notified when you leave them behind.

Where is the product manufactured?

The country of origin is China.

How does the RGB Light Effect work?

The RGB Light Effect Button allows you to customize the lighting on your headphones for a dynamic gaming experience.

  • Single press to cycle through different RGB colors.
  • Long press to turn the lights off.
  • Single press again to turn the lights back on.
